Customer Segmentation Visualization & Advanced Analysis | Task 1

This project focuses on exploring and understanding a customer churn dataset using Microsoft Excel. The analysis includes data overview, sorting, filtering, and basic customer retention metrics to gain initial business insights.

Key Activities
Explored the dataset structure and customer attributes.
Sorted customers based on tenure to identify the newest and longest-serving customers.
Filtered churned customers for focused analysis.
Calculated customer retention and churn rates.
Analyzed tenure distribution, including highest and lowest tenure customers.
Derived business insights related to customer loyalty and retention opportunities.
Skills Demonstrated
Data Cleaning & Exploration
Sorting & Filtering
Excel Formulas (COUNTIF, COUNTA, MAX, MIN, AVERAGE)
Customer Churn Analysis
Business Insight Generation
Data Presentation & Reporting
Key Findings
Customer Retention Rate: 73.46%
Customer Churn Rate: 26.54%
Average Customer Tenure: 32.37 months
362 customers remained with the company for the maximum tenure of 72 months.
Nearly 1 in 4 customers churned, highlighting opportunities for improved retention strategies.
Tools Used
Microsoft Excel / WPS Sheets

This analysis serves as the foundation for advanced customer segmentation, churn visualization, and retention strategy development in subsequent tasks.
